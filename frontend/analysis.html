Accessibility Tips

  • Ensure color contrast ratios meet WCAG 2.1 AA standards (4.5:1 for normal text, 3:1 for large text)
  • Use fonts that are at least 18pt (24px) for poster text to ensure readability from a distance
  • Provide alternative text descriptions for diagrams and visual data
  • Maintain high-resolution images (at least 300 DPI) for print materials
  • Structure content with clear headings and a logical reading order
